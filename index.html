What is Affordable Housing?

A home you can actually afford.

Affordable Housing is priced so that residents can afford other essential living costs — food, clothing, transport, medical care and education. Not just rent.

Affordable housing is for low to moderate income earners who earn too much for social housing but struggle with private rental. Income thresholds vary by household size and location. Contact us for a full eligibility assessment.
Rent is typically set at 25–30% of household gross income, ensuring it remains genuinely affordable. Our team works with each tenant to confirm their eligibility and appropriate rent level.

Who we are

Leaders in affordable housing property management

EchoRealty is part of the Evolve Housing group of companies, which includes one of Australia's leading not-for-profit Community Housing Providers (CHPs). Together, we manage the largest portfolio of affordable housing in NSW.

Our business model combines high quality property management with the objective of creating positive social impact. The revenue we generate funds community programs, expands housing access and supports vulnerable Australians.

Income thresholds vary by household size, composition, and property location. As a general guide, affordable housing is designed for households earning below the median income for their area. Contact our team for a personalised eligibility assessment.
Log into your Tenant Portal at client.propertytree.com to submit a maintenance request. For urgent after-hours emergencies, call our emergency maintenance line on 1800 693 246. We respond to urgent requests within 4 hours and routine requests within 2 business days.
