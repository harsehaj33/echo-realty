Affordable Housing

Bridging the housing gap

Affordable housing offers secure, quality homes for low to moderate income earners — filling the gap between social housing and the private market.

What is Affordable Housing?

A home you can actually afford.

Affordable Housing is priced so that residents can afford other essential living costs including food, clothing, transport, medical care and education — not just rent.

The gap
Social Housing
Affordable 🏠
Private Market
Lowest incomeMarket rate
Affordable housing is available to low to moderate income earners who earn too much to qualify for social housing but struggle to afford private rental market prices. Income thresholds vary by household size and location. Contact us for a full eligibility assessment.
Rent for affordable housing properties is typically set at 25–30% of a household's gross income, ensuring it remains genuinely affordable. Our team works with each tenant to confirm their eligibility and appropriate rent level.
EchoRealty manages properties across New South Wales (NSW), Victoria (VIC), and the Australian Capital Territory (ACT). Our largest portfolio is in Greater Sydney, NSW, where we are the largest affordable housing provider.
To apply, first check our available listings and confirm you meet the eligibility criteria. Then contact our team to start your application. We'll guide you through the full process — from income verification to inspection and lease signing.
